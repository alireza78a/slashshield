SlashShield is a GenLayer prototype for validator downtime/slashing claim decisions. It evaluates public outage evidence and policy exclusions to return a bounded APPROVED or REJECTED payout-eligibility result.

Scope boundary:

- Prototype only.
- Synthetic evidence.
- Not real-money insurance.
- No live validator feed.
- No production settlement.
